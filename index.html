About

Hello, my name is Benji Antolin! I started creating maps at Eastern Washington University (EWU) where I discovered that my hobby and admiration of maps and cartogrpahy could be translated into a career. After completing a Bachelor degree in Economics with Math and a certificate in GIS from EWU, I was hired by Oregon State Univerisity (OSU) Extension as a Faculty Research Assistant specializing in GIS and Data analytics and processing.

As a Faculty member of OSU I have had opportunity to utilize graduate level courses to advance my professional skill set. I have always been interested in dynamic and interactive maps and have had the opportunity to study and work along side Geovisualization Professor, Bo Zhao, in Wep Mapping and Geovisual Analytics. Through my experience, I have been trained to use HTML, CSS, Javascript and Javascripts libraries to build interactive geovisual web applications using open source tools.

As a professional, I strive to create customized web applications to fit stakeholders needs. If you have a dataset or multiple datasets that you would like visualized on a geovisual web platform, I can provide ongoing consultations, maintenance, and updates.
